About Us

Sadi-Ann's Little Cakery Story

Sadi-Ann's Little Cakery has been growing their neighborhood cakery across the world. We stand by our mission statement of using creative flavors, quality ingredients, and friendly service to provide the finest cake experience worthy of returning and recommending.
Baking the most delicious cupcakes and cakes is what Sadi-Ann's Little Cakery is all about. All freshly made to order using local and homemade ingredients at every opportunity makes each one even more special.There is always time for cakes and there is a flavour for everyone!
